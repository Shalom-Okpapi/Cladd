Before You Search

Finding Your Model Number

Your power bank's model number is the key to finding the right pouch. Here's where to look.

On the Device Itself

Check the back or bottom of your power bank. There is usually a sticker or embossed text that reads something like "Model:" or "PB-" followed by numbers and letters. That's your model number.

On the Original Packaging

The box your power bank came in will have the model number printed on it — often near the barcode on the back or bottom of the box.

In Your Phone's Accessory App

Some power banks from brands like Anker and Xiaomi have companion apps. Your model number is usually listed under Device Info or About.

On Your Receipt or Order Confirmation

If you bought the power bank online, your order confirmation or receipt will list the exact model name and number.

Don't guess your model. An incorrect size will result in a pouch that won't snap on correctly. Take a minute to confirm before ordering.

Measure to Be Sure

Size Guide

If your exact model isn't listed but your brand is supported, you may be able to find a compatible fit by measuring your power bank and matching it to our size chart.

Cladd Size Length (mm) Width (mm) Depth (mm)
XS 85–95 55–65 12–16
S 100–115 60–70 14–18
M 120–140 65–75 14–20
L 145–165 70–80 16–22
XL 165–185 75–85 18–25

Measure your power bank with a ruler or tape measure. Match your dimensions to the size range above. If your dimensions fall between two sizes, choose the larger one for a secure fit.

Partial support means we have pouches for some models from that brand but not all. Use the model selector on the product page to see if your specific model is covered. If it's not, submit it below and we'll add it to our roadmap.

If the correct size was available at the time of ordering and you selected the wrong one, we're unable to exchange it as the pouch is made to order. Please double-check your model or dimensions before ordering. If you're unsure, contact us before placing your order and we'll help you find the right fit.

Cladd pouches are sized to fit the bare power bank only. If your power bank already has a separate case or sleeve on it, the Cladd pouch may not snap on correctly. We recommend removing any existing accessories before fitting the Cladd pouch.
