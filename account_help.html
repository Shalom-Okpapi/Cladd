Can't Get In?

Login Issues

If you're having trouble logging in to your Cladd account, work through these steps in order.

Check Your Email Address

Make sure you're using the exact email address you registered with. Cladd accounts are email-specific — logging in with a different address will not work even if the password is correct.

Check Caps Lock

Passwords are case-sensitive. Make sure Caps Lock is off before typing your password.

Clear Your Browser Cache

Cached login data can sometimes interfere. Try clearing your browser's cookies and cache, then log in again. You can also try a different browser or incognito mode.

Reset Your Password

If you've tried the above and still can't log in, reset your password. Go to the password reset page.

Account Suspended

If you receive a message saying your account has been suspended, contact us directly. Suspensions are typically related to policy violations and can be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

Forgotten Password

Resetting Your Password

How to Reset

  1. Go to the Password Reset page
  2. Enter the email address on your account
  3. Check your inbox for a reset link — it expires in 30 minutes
  4. Click the link and set a new password
  5. Log in with your new password

Didn't receive the reset email? Check your spam folder. If it's not there after 5 minutes, try again or contact support.

Choosing a Strong Password

A strong Cladd password should be at least 8 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using your name, email address, or common words.

Profile Details

Changing Your Email or Username

Email Address

You can update your email address from your Account Settings page. After updating, a verification link will be sent to your new email — click it to confirm the change. Your old email remains active until you confirm.

Username

Your username can be changed from your Edit Profile page. Note that your old username becomes available for other users to claim immediately after you change it.

Artists: Changing your username changes your shop URL. Update any links you've shared externally after making the change.

Your Data

Privacy Settings

What You Can Control

  • Marketing emails — unsubscribe at any time from any marketing email or from your account settings
  • Order notifications — manage which order updates you receive by email
  • Profile visibility — artists can control whether their shop is publicly listed

Downloading Your Data

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data Cladd holds about you. To request a data export, contact us with the subject "Data Export Request". We'll process your request within 30 days.

Deleting Your Data

If you close your account, your personal data is deleted or anonymised within 90 days. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Leaving Cladd

Closing Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Before you close your account, here's what to know:

  • Any pending orders will still be fulfilled — account closure does not cancel active orders
  • Artist earnings that have not yet been withdrawn will be paid out before closure
  • Your designs will be removed from the marketplace
  • Your account data will be deleted within 90 days
  • Your username will become available to other users immediately

Account closure is permanent. You cannot reopen a closed account. If you'd just like a break, consider simply logging out and returning later.

To close your account, go to Account Settings and select "Close Account" at the bottom of the page, or contact us for assistance.
