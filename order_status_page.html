What does it mean?

Understanding Your Order Status

Each status below tells you exactly where your order is in the process.

Order Received

We've got your order and it's being reviewed. This usually takes a few minutes to a few hours.

In Production

Your design is being printed and the pouch is being assembled. Production typically takes 2–5 business days.

Shipped

Your order is on its way. A tracking number will arrive in your email within 24 hours of shipment.

Out for Delivery

Your package is with a local courier and will be delivered today. Stay close!

Delivered

Your order has been delivered. If you can't locate it, check with neighbours or your building reception. Contact us within 7 days if it's missing.

Delayed

Your order is taking longer than expected — usually due to customs, courier issues, or high demand. We'll notify you by email. You can also contact us for an update.

Common Questions

Order FAQs

Tracking numbers are sent by email within 24 hours of your order shipping. Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's still not there after 7 business days from your order date, contact our support team.

Address changes can only be made while your order is still in the "Order Received" or "In Production" stage. Once shipped, the address cannot be changed. Contact us immediately if you need to update your address.

Check with neighbours, your building reception, or any safe spots couriers commonly leave parcels. If you still can't locate it within 24 hours of the delivery notification, contact us and we'll investigate with the courier on your behalf.

Orders can be cancelled while in the "Order Received" stage only. Once production has started, cancellations are no longer possible as your item is already being made. Contact us as quickly as possible if you need to cancel.
