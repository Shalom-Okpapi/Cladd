Who we are

We were tired of carrying things
that said nothing about us.

Cladd was founded by Shalom Okpapi — not in a boardroom, not with funding, but with a cracked power bank and a clear frustration. Power banks are one of the most carried objects on the planet. And for years, nobody thought to make them personal.

"I wanted protection, style, and personality — all in one. The market offered nothing that came close. So I decided to build it myself. Now look at where we are." — Shalom Okpapi, Founder

Our mission

Protect. Style. Charge.

Our mission is to give every person on the planet a way to carry their power bank with intention. We believe your everyday accessories should reflect who you are — not just what they do.

That's why we built a marketplace where independent artists design the pouches, and you choose the one that resonates with you. Every purchase supports a real creative. Every pouch tells a real story. We're not building a product — we're building a culture around functional art.

Open to everyone

Who Can Join?

Anyone — and for free. We invite creators from anywhere in the world to pour their identity into their designs and earn from it. Or come as a customer — explore our marketplace and find art that finally looks like you.
