The Process

How shipping works on Cladd.

Every Cladd pouch is made to order. That means production happens first — then shipping. Here's what that looks like end to end:

1

Order Placed

Your order enters our system and is queued for production.

2

Production

Your design is printed and the pouch is assembled. This takes 2–5 business days.

3

Dispatched

Your order is handed to a courier and a tracking number is emailed to you.

4

Delivered

Your package arrives at your door. Carry it with pride.

Estimated Times

Delivery Times

These are estimated delivery windows from the date your order is dispatched — not from when you place your order. Add 2–5 business days for production.

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery
Standard (Domestic) 5–10 business days
Express (Domestic) 2–5 business days
Standard (International) 10–20 business days
Express (International) 5–10 business days

Peak periods: During high demand periods such as holidays or major sales events, production and delivery times may be longer. We recommend ordering at least 3 weeks in advance for time-sensitive gifts.

Global Delivery

International Orders

Cladd ships worldwide. Here's what to know if you're ordering outside our home market.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs fees, import duties, or taxes imposed by your country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in the Cladd order total. We have no control over these fees and cannot predict their amount.

Customs Delays

International packages occasionally experience delays at customs. This is outside our control but we'll always do our best to help you resolve any issues. Contact us if your order is significantly delayed.

Restricted Countries

There are some countries we're currently unable to ship to due to international shipping restrictions. If your country is unavailable at checkout, please contact us — we may be able to help.

Find Your Package

Tracking Your Order

When Will I Receive a Tracking Number?

A tracking number is emailed to you within 24 hours of your order being dispatched. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder before contacting us.

How to Track

Log in to your Cladd account, go to Order History, and click Track Package next to your order. You can also use the tracking number directly on the courier's website for real-time updates.

Tracking Not Updating?

It can take up to 48 hours after dispatch for tracking information to appear on the courier's system. If it's been more than 3 business days and tracking still shows no movement, contact our support team.
