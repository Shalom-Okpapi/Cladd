Buying a Gift Card

A Cladd gift card is the easiest way to give someone the freedom to choose exactly what represents them. No guessing their taste — let them pick it themselves.

How to Buy

  1. Select your preferred gift card amount above
  2. Enter the recipient's name and email address
  3. Add a personal message (optional)
  4. Choose a delivery date — send instantly or schedule it for a specific day
  5. Complete your purchase — the gift card arrives in the recipient's inbox

Gift cards are delivered by email only. No physical card is sent. The email contains the unique code used at checkout.

For the Recipient

Redeeming a Gift Card

Got a Cladd gift card? Here's how to use it.

  1. Open your gift card email and copy the unique code
  2. Shop the Cladd marketplace and add items to your cart
  3. At checkout, enter your code in the "Coupon or Gift Card" field
  4. The gift card value is deducted from your order total instantly
  5. If your order exceeds the gift card value, pay the remainder with any accepted payment method

Gift cards never expire. Use it whenever you're ready — no pressure, no deadline.

How Much Left?

Checking Your Balance

Log in to your Cladd account and go to Account Settings → Gift Cards. Your current balance is shown alongside your transaction history for that card.

You can also enter your code at checkout — the remaining balance will be shown before you confirm your order.

Need help finding your balance? Contact us with your gift card code and we'll look it up.

Good to Know

Gift Card Terms

  • Valid for purchases on Cladd only
  • Cannot be exchanged for cash
  • Never expire
  • Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced — treat the code like cash
  • Cannot be used to purchase other gift cards
  • Partial redemption is supported — remaining balance is retained
  • Non-refundable once purchased
  • Cladd is not responsible for cards sent to an incorrect email address provided at purchase
