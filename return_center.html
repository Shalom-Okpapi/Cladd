Before You Submit

What Qualifies for a Return

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for the following reasons only.

Defective Product

Broken snap, peeling print, or any manufacturing defect on arrival.

Wrong Item Received

You received a different design, size, or product than what you ordered.

Damaged in Transit

Visible damage caused by shipping. Keep the original packaging for your case.

Significantly Not as Described

The product received is materially different from what was shown on the product page.

Change of Mind

Every pouch is made to order. We cannot accept returns for change of mind.

Incorrect Size Selected

Please verify your power bank model before ordering. Size selection errors are not eligible.

Requests After 30 Days

Return requests made more than 30 days after delivery will not be accepted.

For the full policy, visit our Returns & Exchanges page.

What Happens Next

The Return Process

1. We Review Your Request

Our team reviews every return request within 2 business days. You will receive an email with our decision and next steps.

2. Send Us Photos

For defective, damaged, or incorrect items, reply to our confirmation email with clear photos. This is the fastest way to get your return approved.

3. Ship It Back if Required

If a physical return is required and your case is approved, we will send you a prepaid return label. You will never pay for return shipping on eligible items.

4. Refund or Replacement

Once received and inspected, your preferred resolution will be processed within 3 to 7 business days.

Do not send items back without contacting us first. Unauthorised returns may not be processed.
