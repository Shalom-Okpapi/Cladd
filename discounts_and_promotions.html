Ways to Save

Types of Discounts

Cladd offers several ways to reduce what you pay. Here's a breakdown of every discount type available.

Coupon Codes

Percentage or flat-amount codes entered at checkout. Found in our newsletter, on social media, and on this page.

Welcome Discount

New to Cladd? Get 10% off your first order automatically when you sign up. Applied at checkout — no code needed.

Referral Rewards

Refer a friend and earn $2 every time they make a qualifying purchase. Learn more about Refer & Earn.

Seasonal Sales

We run sales during major periods — Black Friday, New Year, and other key dates. Subscribe to our newsletter to be first to know.

Free Shipping

All domestic orders over $60 qualify for free standard shipping automatically. No code required.

Step by Step

How to Apply a Coupon Code

  1. Add your chosen items to your cart
  2. Go to your cart and click Proceed to Checkout
  3. Find the "Coupon or Gift Card" field in the order summary
  4. Enter your code exactly as shown — codes are case-sensitive
  5. Click Apply — the discount is reflected in your total instantly
  6. Complete your order

Codes are case-sensitive. Type or paste them exactly as shown — "cladd15" and "CLADD15" are not the same.

The Fine Print

Coupon Rules

  • Only one coupon code can be applied per order
  • Codes cannot be combined with other promotions unless explicitly stated
  • Welcome discount codes are valid for first-time purchases only — one per account
  • Codes cannot be applied retroactively to completed orders
  • Cladd reserves the right to expire or disable any coupon code at any time
  • Coupon discounts apply to the product subtotal only — not to shipping costs
  • If an order is refunded, the coupon value is not returned
