Welcome to Pouchify! 👋

Follow these steps to set up your shop and start selling your art to the world.

Add New Work
Step 1

Create Products

Choose Your Product

  • Select a product from Pouchify's product listing.
  • Customise product colours, styles, and design placement.

Upload Your Designs

  • Upload your original art to Pouchify.
  • Accepted file types: JPEG, PNG, SVG — minimum 300px, recommended 900px. You'll need at least 3 designs for your shop to go live.

Set Your Prices

  • Set your own prices for products (if eligible).
  • Understand base prices, royalty rates, and profit margins.
Step 2

Sell Art & Get Paid

Sell Your Products

  • Pouchify produces and ships products directly to your customers.
  • Products are sold through Pouchify's website, social media, and other channels.

Earn Royalties

  • Earn a percentage of the sale price for each product sold.
  • Royalty rates vary depending on product type and pricing tier.

Get Paid

  • Payments are made via Bank Transfer, PayPal, Payoneer, or Grey.
  • Minimum payment threshold and payment schedules apply.
Step 3

Your Account

Verification

  • Your account will be verified once you've completed the steps above.
  • Verification can take up to 3–5 business days.
  • Your account will be classified as Basic or Premium after review — you'll receive a confirmation email once it's done.

Now You're All Set! 😎

Your shop is ready. Start uploading your best work and let the world discover your art.
