Cladd

For independent artists

Your art. The world's carry.

Set up your Cladd shop in three steps. Upload your designs, sell to the world, and get paid. No inventory. No shipping. Just you and your art.

01 First

Create

Build Your Products

Choose Your Product

Browse Cladd's product range and select the canvas for your art — power bank pouches, phone pouches, stickers, and more.

Upload Your Designs

Upload your original artwork. Accepted: PNG, JPG, SVG at 300 DPI minimum. You need at least 3 designs for your shop to go live.

Read the Design Tips guide

Set Your Prices

You control your selling price. Cladd deducts a platform fee — everything above it is your royalty. Price your work like the art it is.

02 Then

Sell

Sell Art. Get Paid.

We Handle Everything

When a customer orders your design, Cladd produces, packages, and ships it directly to them. You do nothing except create.

Earn Royalties

Every sale earns you a royalty. Track everything in your dashboard in real time.

Withdraw Anytime

Earnings accumulate in your Cladd wallet. Withdraw via Bank Transfer, PayPal, Payoneer, or Grey once you hit the minimum threshold.

03 Finally

Verified

Your Account Tier

Verification

After you complete setup, your account is reviewed. Verification takes 3 to 5 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once done.

Basic or Premium

Your account is classified after review. Basic is free to start. Premium unlocks homepage placement, priority review, and dedicated support.
