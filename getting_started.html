The Short Version

What is Cladd?

Cladd is a print-on-demand marketplace with one founding idea — that the things you carry every day should say something about who you are.

We make it possible for independent artists to sell original designs on protective pouches for power banks and other everyday accessories. And we make it possible for shoppers to find art that actually represents them — not just a generic product anyone could buy.

Everyone wins. Artists get paid. Shoppers get something meaningful. The world gets a little more personal.

Step One

Creating Your Account

  1. Go to the Sign Up page
  2. Choose your account type — Shopper or Creator
  3. Enter your email address and choose a password
  4. If you're creating a shop, enter your shop name
  5. Click Create My Account
  6. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link

Already have an account? Log in here.

For Shoppers

Start Shopping

Finding the right design is the whole experience. Here's how to make the most of it.

Browse the Marketplace

Go to Shop to browse all available designs. Use the filter bar to narrow by product type, style, or artist. The search bar lets you find specific themes or keywords.

Save Your Favourites

When you find a design that resonates, tap the heart icon to save it to your Favorites. Come back to it later or share it with someone who'd love it.

Placing an Order

  1. Select your product and confirm your power bank model
  2. Add to cart
  3. Review your cart and apply any coupon codes
  4. Enter your shipping address and choose a delivery method
  5. Pay securely and receive your order confirmation by email

After You Order

Your order enters production within hours. You'll receive a tracking number by email once it ships. Track your order anytime from the Order Status page.

For Creators

Start Selling

Cladd gives independent artists a platform to sell original designs on physical products without managing inventory, production, or shipping. We handle all of that. You focus on creating.

Setting Up Your Shop

  1. Sign up with a Creator account at Signup
  2. Complete your profile — add a photo, bio, and shop description
  3. Go to your Dashboard and click Add Work
  4. Upload your first design and select which products to apply it to
  5. Set your price and submit for review
  6. Once approved, your design goes live in the marketplace

Your shop is free to set up. Cladd only earns when you earn — through a platform fee deducted from each sale.

Your Designs

Uploading Designs

Getting your file right before uploading ensures your design prints exactly as intended. Here are the key requirements:

  • Format: PNG (preferred), JPG, or SVG
  • Resolution: 300 DPI minimum
  • Canvas size: At least 3000 × 3000 px for pouches
  • Colour mode: RGB
  • File size: Under 25MB
  • Safe zone: Keep important elements 5mm from all edges

For a complete guide, read our Design Tips page.

Originality is required. Only upload artwork you own or have full rights to. Designs containing copyrighted characters or logos will be rejected. See our Copyright & IP page for details.

Getting Paid

Royalties & Payouts

Every time your design sells, you earn a royalty. Here's how it works:

How Royalties Are Calculated

You set your own selling price in your dashboard. Cladd deducts a platform fee that covers production, fulfilment, and platform costs. What remains after the fee is your royalty — paid directly to you.

Minimum Payout Threshold

Earnings accumulate in your Cladd wallet. You can request a payout once your balance reaches the minimum withdrawal threshold shown in your dashboard.

Payout Methods

Withdraw your earnings via wire transfer, Payoneer, PayPal, or Grey. Processing times vary by method but typically complete within 3–7 business days.

Grow Your Shop

Promoting Your Work

Cladd gives you the platform — you bring the audience. Here's how to drive traffic to your shop.

Share Your Shop Link

Your Cladd shop has a unique URL. Share it everywhere — Instagram bio, Pinterest boards, TikTok profile, WhatsApp. Every person who discovers you through your own promotion is a customer you brought in.

Use Pinterest Specifically

Pinterest is the highest-converting social platform for POD (print-on-demand) products. Create boards organised by theme or aesthetic and pin your designs consistently. Pinterest content has a long lifespan — a pin from months ago can still drive sales today.

Build a Series

Shops with a consistent visual theme or series perform better than scattered portfolios. When someone loves one design, they look at your other work. Give them more to love.

Use the Referral Programme

Share your unique referral link as well as your shop link. You'll earn $2 for every qualifying purchase made through your referral — on top of your design royalties. Learn more about Refer & Earn.
