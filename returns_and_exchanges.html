Our Policy

What's eligible for a return?

Because every Cladd pouch is made to order specifically for you, our return policy is limited to the situations below. We take quality seriously — if something arrived wrong, we'll make it right.

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for any of the following reasons:


Defective Product

The pouch arrived with a manufacturing defect — broken snap, peeling print, or structural damage.

Wrong Item Received

You received a different design, size, or product than what you ordered.

Significantly Not As Described

The product you received is materially different from what was shown on the product page.

Damaged in Transit

The item arrived visibly damaged due to shipping. Keep the original packaging — we may need photos.

What's not eligible?

Change of Mind

Because every pouch is made to order, we can't accept returns for change of mind or buyer's remorse.

Incorrect Size Selected

Please double-check your power bank model before ordering. Returns for incorrect size selections are not accepted.

Used or Damaged Items

Items that have been used or damaged after delivery are not eligible for return.

Requests After 30 Days

Return requests made more than 30 days after your delivery date will not be accepted.

Step by Step

How to request a return.

1. Contact Us First

Go to our Contact Page and select "Returns & Exchanges" as your topic. Include your order number and a clear description of the issue.

2. Send Us Photos

For defective, damaged, or incorrect items, attach clear photos of the product and the issue. This helps us resolve your case faster and avoids unnecessary back-and-forth.

3. We Review Your Case

Our team reviews all return requests within 2 business days. We'll contact you by email with a decision and next steps.

4. Ship It Back (if required)

If your return is approved and a physical return is required, we'll provide a prepaid return label. You'll never pay for return shipping on eligible items.

Do not send items back without contacting us first. Unauthorised returns may not be processed.

Exchanges

If you received a defective or incorrect item and would prefer an exchange rather than a refund, just let us know when you submit your return request. We'll replace the item with the correct product at no additional cost.

We do not currently support exchanges for a different design or size when the original order was placed correctly — but we're working on making this possible in future.
