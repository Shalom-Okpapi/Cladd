The Model

How selling on Cladd works.

Cladd is a print-on-demand marketplace. That means you never hold stock, handle packaging, or deal with shipping. Here's the full flow from your end:

1

You create a design

Upload original artwork from your computer or device. PNG, JPG, or SVG at 300 DPI minimum.

2

You set your price

Choose your selling price. Cladd deducts a platform fee; the rest is your royalty.

3

We review and publish your design

Our team checks every design before it goes live. Review takes 1–3 business days.

4

A customer finds it and orders

Your design appears in the marketplace. When a customer buys it, we receive the order.

5

We produce and ship

Cladd handles all production, packaging, and worldwide delivery. You do nothing.

6

You get paid

Your royalty is added to your Cladd wallet. Withdraw anytime once you reach the minimum threshold.

Getting Live

Shop Setup

Your Shop Profile

A complete profile builds trust with customers before they even see your designs. Fill in every field:

  • Shop name — your brand. Choose something memorable and consistent with your style.
  • Profile photo — a photo, logo, or piece of your art. Shops with photos consistently outperform blank profiles.
  • Bio — two to three sentences about who you are and what drives your work. Keep it human.
  • Shop description — what your shop specialises in. Helps customers understand your aesthetic at a glance.

Adding Your First Design

  1. Go to your Dashboard and click Add Work
  2. Upload your design file
  3. Select which products to apply it to
  4. Preview the mockup — check alignment and that nothing important is near the edges
  5. Write a title and description for your design
  6. Set your price
  7. Submit for review
Your Earnings

Pricing Your Work

You have full control over your selling price. Here's how to think about it.

Base Cost + Your Royalty = Selling Price

Cladd charges a base production cost per product. Your selling price must cover that base cost. Everything above it is your royalty. You can see the exact breakdown in your dashboard when setting a price.

Don't Price Too Low

Underpricing makes your work look less valuable and earns you less per sale. Customers on Cladd are buying identity and art — not a bargain. Price accordingly.

Don't Price Too High

Very high prices relative to comparable designs in the marketplace will reduce your conversion rate. Check what similar designs are priced at and position yours within a competitive range.

You can update your price at any time from your dashboard. Changes apply to new orders — existing orders are not affected.

Before You Go Live

The Review Process

Every design submitted to Cladd is reviewed by our team before it goes live. This protects customers, protects artists, and keeps the marketplace quality high.

What We Check

  • File resolution and quality — minimum 300 DPI
  • Originality — the design must be your own work
  • No copyrighted characters, logos, or trademarks you don't own
  • No offensive, hateful, or prohibited content
  • Accurate product and design description

Review Timeline

Most designs are reviewed within 1–3 business days. You'll receive an email when your design is approved or if it requires changes.

If Your Design Is Rejected

We'll tell you exactly why. Most rejections are for fixable issues — low resolution, a borderline element, or a description mismatch. Fix the issue and resubmit. If you believe a rejection was made in error, contact us to request a review.

Getting Paid

Payouts

When Do I Get Paid?

Royalties are added to your Cladd wallet after each order is successfully delivered and the return window closes. You can request a withdrawal once your wallet balance reaches the minimum threshold shown in your dashboard.

Payout Methods

  • Wire Transfer — direct to your bank account
  • Payoneer — ideal for international sellers
  • PayPal — fast and widely available
  • Grey — great for Nigerian and African sellers

Processing Times

Withdrawal requests are processed within 3–7 business days depending on your chosen method. You'll receive a confirmation email when your payout is on its way.

Make sure your payout details are accurate before requesting a withdrawal. Cladd is not responsible for funds sent to incorrect account details provided by the seller.

The Rules

Seller Policies

Your Content

You retain full ownership of your original artwork. By listing on Cladd you grant us a non-exclusive licence to display, reproduce, and sell products featuring your design. We never claim creative ownership of anything you upload.

Prohibited Content

  • Sexually explicit or nude content
  • Content promoting hate, discrimination, or violence
  • Designs infringing on another party's intellectual property
  • Content containing personal information of real individuals without consent

Account Suspension

Repeated or serious violations of these policies may result in account suspension. Outstanding earnings at the time of suspension are reviewed case by case. See our full Seller Agreement for complete terms.
