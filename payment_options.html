How to Pay

Accepted Payment Methods

Cladd accepts the following payment methods at checkout. All transactions are processed securely.

Credit & Debit Cards

Visa, Mastercard, and Verve accepted. Card details are encrypted and never stored by Cladd.

PayPal

Pay with your PayPal balance, linked bank account, or PayPal credit where available.

Apple Pay

Available on Safari on iOS and macOS. Complete your purchase with Face ID or Touch ID.

Google Pay

Available on Chrome and Android. Pay instantly using your saved Google Pay method.

Cladd Gift Cards

Enter your gift card code at checkout to apply the balance to your order. Learn more.

Coupon Codes

Apply a valid coupon code at checkout to reduce your order total. See active promotions.

We do not accept: Cryptocurrency, bank transfers, cheques, or cash on delivery.

Your Safety

Payment Security

Every transaction on Cladd is protected by industry-standard security measures.

SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and Cladd is encrypted using SSL (Secure Sockets Layer). Look for the padlock icon in your browser's address bar.

We Never Store Card Details

Cladd does not store your full card number, CVV, or expiry date. Payment processing is handled by our certified payment provider. We receive only a transaction confirmation.

3D Secure

Many card transactions are verified using 3D Secure — an extra layer of authentication from your bank. You may be asked to approve the payment in your banking app before your order is confirmed.

If you ever receive a suspicious email or message claiming to be from Cladd and asking for payment outside the platform, do not pay and report it to us immediately.

Pricing

Currency & Pricing

All prices on Cladd are displayed in USD (US Dollars) by default. Your bank or payment provider will convert the charge to your local currency at the current exchange rate at the time of payment.

Cladd is not responsible for currency conversion fees charged by your bank. These vary by provider — check with your bank if you're unsure what applies to you.

Something Wrong?

Billing Issues

Payment Declined

If your payment is declined, try the following:

  • Check that your card details are entered correctly
  • Ensure your billing address matches what your bank has on file
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Contact your bank — some banks block international transactions by default
  • Try a different payment method

Charged but No Confirmation

If you were charged but did not receive an order confirmation email, check your spam folder first. If it's not there, contact us with the transaction date and approximate amount and we'll look into it immediately.

Duplicate Charges

Duplicate charges occasionally appear as pending transactions that resolve automatically within 3–5 business days. If the charge persists, contact us and we'll work with our payment provider to resolve it.
