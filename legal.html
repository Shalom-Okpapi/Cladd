Privacy Policy

How we handle your data.

Cladd ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used when you visit or use cladd.com and any related services.

Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Name and email address when you create an account
  • Billing and shipping address when you place an order
  • Payment information — processed securely by our payment provider. Cladd does not store card details.
  • Profile information such as username, bio, and shop name (for artists)
  • Any content you upload including designs, images, and messages

Information collected automatically

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Referral source (how you found us)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To communicate with you about your account or orders
  • To improve the Cladd platform and your experience on it

  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not share it for advertising purposes outside of Cladd.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our Contact Page.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymise your data within 90 days unless we are required by law to retain it longer.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Cladd after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  • You grant Cladd a non-exclusive licence to display, reproduce, and sell products featuring your design
  • You retain full ownership of your original artwork
  • Cladd does not claim creative ownership of anything you upload

Do not upload designs that include copyrighted characters, logos, or artwork that you do not own. Violations will result in immediate removal and potential account suspension.

Earnings & Payouts

Artists earn a royalty on every sale of their design. Royalty rates are set within your seller dashboard. Payouts are processed once your balance reaches the minimum withdrawal threshold. Cladd deducts a platform fee from each sale to cover production, fulfilment, and platform costs.
