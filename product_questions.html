Our Flagship Product

The Cladd Power Bank Pouch

Cladd was built around one product that didn't exist — a protective snap-fit pouch for your power bank. Here's exactly what it is and what makes it different.

360° Protection

The pouch wraps your power bank on all sides — top, bottom, front, back, and edges. Nothing is left exposed.

Snap-Fit Design

No zips, no velcro, no fumbling. The pouch snaps securely onto your power bank and holds firm. One click on, one click off.

Port Access Cutout

A peel-back section over the ports lets you charge without removing the pouch. It stays attached — it never peels off completely.

Original Artist Designs

Every pouch is printed with an original design by an independent artist on the Cladd marketplace. No two people need to carry the same one.

What It's Made Of

Materials

Outer Shell

The outer shell is made from a durable thermoplastic polymer — the same family of materials used in high-quality phone cases. It's impact-resistant, lightweight, and holds its shape under pressure.

Print Layer

Designs are applied using a UV-cured dye-sublimation process. This means the print is embedded into the surface rather than sitting on top of it — it won't peel, crack, or fade under normal use.

Inner Lining

The interior is lined with a soft microfibre material to protect your power bank's surface from scratches during everyday use.

Port Cutout Mechanism

The peel-back port access section is made from a flexible silicone hinge that allows repeated opening and closing without weakening or tearing. It is permanently attached to the pouch shell.

Vegan & cruelty-free. No animal-derived materials are used in any Cladd product.

Will It Fit?

Sizing & Compatibility

Cladd pouches are manufactured in sizes matched to specific power bank models and dimensions. Getting the right fit is important — a pouch that's too loose won't protect, and one that's too tight won't snap on correctly.

How to Find Your Size

  1. Check your power bank's model number — usually on a sticker on the back or bottom
  2. Use the model selector on the product page to find compatible pouches
  3. If your model isn't listed, visit our Can't Find My Model page

Size by Dimensions

If you can't find your model, measure your power bank's length, width, and depth in millimetres and use our size guide on the product page to find the closest match.

Sizing is non-returnable if the correct size was available and a different one was selected. Double-check your model before ordering. If your model isn't listed, contact us before purchasing.

How It Looks

Print Quality

We print every Cladd pouch using professional-grade UV printing equipment. Here's what to expect.

Colour Accuracy

Printed colours may appear slightly different from what you see on screen — this is normal across all print-on-demand products. Screens display in RGB light; physical prints use pigment. Darker designs tend to be most accurate. Very light or pastel designs may appear slightly more saturated in print.

Resolution

We print at 300 DPI minimum. Designs uploaded below this resolution will be rejected before production. If you're an artist, see our Design Tips page for full file requirements.

Defective Prints

If your pouch arrives with a visible print defect — banding, blurring, or missing areas — this is covered under our return policy. Photograph the defect and contact us within 30 days of delivery.

Keeping It Fresh

Care Instructions

Cleaning

Wipe down your Cladd pouch with a slightly damp microfibre cloth. Avoid abrasive materials, solvents, alcohol wipes, or harsh cleaning products — these can damage the print surface over time.

What to Avoid

  • Do not submerge in water — the pouch is not waterproof
  • Do not expose to prolonged direct sunlight — UV exposure can fade the print over time
  • Do not use sharp objects near the surface
  • Do not force the snap mechanism — if it's not clicking, check your model size

Port Cutout Care

The peel-back port section is designed for daily use. Open and close it normally — avoid pulling it at extreme angles or stretching it past its natural range of motion.
