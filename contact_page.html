What happens next

After you send.

1. You'll get a confirmation

As soon as your message is received, we'll send a confirmation to your email address.

A real person on our team reads every message. No bots. No auto-replies after the confirmation.

Most messages are responded to within 24–48 hours on weekdays. Complex issues like order disputes may take a little longer — we'll let you know if that's the case.
