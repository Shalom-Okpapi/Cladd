Frequently Asked Questions

Cladd pouches are designed with breathable materials and passive venting so heat dissipates naturally. Your power bank stays protected without trapping warmth.

Standard shipping: 5–10 business days. Express: 2–5 business days. International: 10–20 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for defective or incorrect items. Item must be unused and in its original packaging. See our Returns & Exchanges page for full details.

Log in to your account, go to Order History, and click Track Package next to your order. Tracking is usually available 3–5 business days after ordering.

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cladd Gift Cards. Cryptocurrency and bank transfers are not currently supported.

Create a free Cladd account, go to your Dashboard, click "Add Work", upload your original artwork, and submit for review. Once approved your designs go live. Read the full guide.
